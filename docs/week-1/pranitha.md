# Week 1 – Evaluation System

## Task 1: Capability Factors

### 1. Problem Framing Ability
**What it means:**
Ability to clearly understand and define the actual problem before starting to solve it.

---

### 2. Structured Thinking & Communication
**What it means:**
Ability to think logically and explain ideas in a clear and structured manner.

---

### 3. Adaptability & Learning Ability
**What it means:**
Ability to adjust approach when things change and learn from mistakes or feedback.

---

### 4. Attention to Edge Cases
**What it means:**
Ability to think beyond normal scenarios and identify possible failures or exceptions.

---

### 5. Ownership & Execution Discipline
**What it means:**
Taking responsibility for tasks and ensuring consistent effort until completion.
