# Week 1 – Evaluation System

## Task 1: Capability Factors

### 1. Problem Framing Ability
**What it means:**
Ability to clearly understand and define the actual problem before starting to solve it.

### 2. Structured Thinking & Communication
**What it means:**
Ability to think logically and explain ideas in a clear and structured manner.

### 3. Adaptability & Learning Ability
**What it means:**
Ability to adjust approach when things change and learn from mistakes or feedback.

### 4. Attention to Edge Cases
**What it means:**
Ability to think beyond normal scenarios and identify possible failures or exceptions.

### 5. Ownership & Execution Discipline
**What it means:**
Taking responsibility for tasks and ensuring consistent effort until completion.

---

## Task 2: Observation Method

### 1. Problem Framing Ability
- Candidate asks clarifying questions before starting
- Breaks problem into smaller parts
- Identifies missing or unclear information
- Restates the problem in their own words


### 2. Structured Thinking & Communication
- Explains solution step-by-step
- Organizes thoughts before speaking
- Uses simple and clear language
- Keeps explanation logical and easy to follow


### 3. Adaptability & Learning Ability
- Changes approach when initial solution fails
- Accepts feedback and improves
- Does not stick rigidly to one idea
- Learns from mistakes and applies corrections


### 4. Attention to Edge Cases
- Considers unusual or extreme scenarios
- Thinks about possible failures
- Tests solution against different conditions
- Does not assume ideal conditions


### 5. Ownership & Execution Discipline
- Completes assigned tasks without reminders
- Shows consistency in daily work
- Takes responsibility for mistakes
- Follows through until task is finished
