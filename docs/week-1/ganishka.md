## Task 1 – Capability Factors (Behavior-Based)
- **Clarity of Thinking** – Explains ideas step-by-step without confusion  
- **Problem-Solving Approach** – Breaks problems into smaller parts before solving  
- **Handling Uncertainty** – Works even when full information is not available  
- **Decision-Making Ability** – Chooses an approach and justifies it  
- **Communication & Collaboration** – Shares ideas clearly and listens to others  


## Task 2 – Observation Method
- **Clarity of Thinking** → Ask candidate to explain their solution process  
- **Problem-Solving Approach** → Give a real problem and observe how they break it down  
- **Handling Uncertainty** → Provide incomplete data and see how they proceed  
- **Decision-Making Ability** → Present 2 options and observe how they choose  
- **Communication & Collaboration** → Observe discussion in group or interaction  


## Task 3 – Failure Conditions
- Gives vague or generic answers without examples  
- Avoids taking decisions or keeps changing answers  
- Cannot explain their own solution or reasoning  


## Task 4 – Evaluation Process (No Marks/Test)
- Candidate explains approach to a real problem  
- Reviewer asks follow-up questions  
- Observe thinking, not just final answer  
- Check consistency in responses  
- Give feedback based on behavior and reasoning
