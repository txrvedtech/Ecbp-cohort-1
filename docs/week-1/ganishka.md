# WEEK-1
## Task 1 – Capability Factors (Behavior-Based)
- Explains ideas clearly and adapts explanation when questioned  
- Breaks problems logically and identifies key parts before solving  
- Moves forward by making reasonable assumptions when information is missing  
- Takes a clear decision and justifies it with strong reasoning  
- Communicates clearly, listens actively, and builds on others’ inputs  

## Task 2 – Observation Method
- Observe how clearly and confidently the candidate explains their thinking  
- See how they structure and approach a real problem step-by-step  
- Check how they proceed when data is incomplete—do they pause or assume logically  
- Observe how they choose between options and justify trade-offs  
- Watch how they listen, respond, and engage during discussions  

## Task 3 – Failure Conditions
- Gives memorized or generic answers without real thinking  
- Avoids taking decisions or cannot justify their choices  
- Fails to explain their reasoning or breaks under questioning  

## Task 4 – Evaluation Process (No Marks/Test)
- Give a practical situation and let the candidate respond freely  
- Ask deep follow-up questions to challenge their thinking  
- Observe reasoning, assumptions, and decision-making process  
- Check consistency when their ideas are questioned or changed  
- Give feedback based on behaviour, clarity, and thought process
