# Week 1 Tasks

## Task 1: Capability Factors

1. Paying attention to details  
2. Learning from mistakes  
3. Thinking from different perspectives  
4. Clearly expressing their thinking process  
5. Completing tasks without giving up  

---

## Task 2: Observation Method

**Factor: Paying attention to details**  
- Checking if instructions are followed correctly and avoiding small mistakes  

**Factor: Learning from mistakes**  
- Observing whether mistakes are corrected and not repeated  

**Factor: Thinking from different perspectives**  
- Checking if multiple approaches are considered instead of sticking to one  

**Factor: Clearly expressing their thinking process**  
- Seeing if thoughts are explained clearly without confusion  

**Factor: Completing tasks without giving up**  
- Observing whether the task is completed or left incomplete  

---

## Task 3: Failure Conditions

1. Gets distracted and misses the main point  
2. Does not clarify doubts before starting  
3. Gives unclear explanations  

---

## Task 4: Evaluation Process

- Reviewer observes how the candidate solves the problem  
- Understanding is checked by making small changes to the problem  
- Focus is on thinking process, not just the final answer  
- Feedback is given through discussion for improvement
