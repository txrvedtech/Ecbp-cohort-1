Task 1 – Define Capability Factors 

5 factors that define a strong candidate

1. Confidence 
2. Clarity
3. Ability to reason
4. Decision Making
5. Patience

Task 2 - Observation Method

Confidence : Body language , voice.
Clarity : Through text or voice. Deviation from topic indicates lack of clarity.
Ability to reason : Have a supporting statements for every topic mentioned.
Decision Making : Selecting between two options within a time frame.
Patience : If the person is able to wait patiently until the task is fully completed
