Task 1 – Define Capability Factors - 5 factors that define a strong candidate
1. Confidence 
2. Clarity
3. Ability to reason
4. Decision Making
5. Patience

Task 2 - Observation Method
1. Confidence : Body language , communication skills.
2. Clarity : Through text or voice. Deviation from topic indicates lack of clarity.
3. Ability to reason : Have a supporting statements for every topic mentioned.
4. Decision Making : Selecting between two options within a time frame.
5. Patience : If the person is able to wait patiently until the task is fully completed

Task 3 - Failure Conditions - 3 situations where a candidate clearly fails
1. Unwillingness to learn.
2. Intention is to just 'qualify a round'
3. Candidate is not self aware.
